Privacy, Safety & Rules

1. Privacy Policy

At ExploreEra, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information.

1.1 Information We Collect

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Login credentials
  • Booking details (hotels, guides, tours)
  • Communication history

1.2 How We Use Your Information

  • Create and manage user accounts
  • Process bookings and reservations
  • Verify guiders and hotel managers
  • Improve user experience
  • Provide customer support

We do not sell or share your personal data with unauthorized third parties.

1.3 Data Protection

We use secure systems including encrypted passwords, secure logins, and restricted access to protect your data.

2. Safety Policy

2.1 Verified Guiders & Hotel Managers

All Guiders and Hotel Managers must complete a manual verification process before receiving login credentials.

2.2 Traveler Safety

  • Communicate only through official ExploreEra channels
  • Do not share sensitive personal information
  • Report suspicious activity immediately

2.3 Platform Monitoring

ExploreEra reserves the right to monitor activities and suspend accounts involved in fraud or unsafe behavior.

3. Rules & Responsibilities

3.1 User Rules

  • Provide accurate information
  • Use the platform respectfully
  • Follow local laws and regulations

3.2 Guider Rules

  • Maintain professionalism
  • Ensure traveler safety
  • Follow ExploreEra policies

3.3 Hotel Manager Rules

  • Provide correct hotel information
  • Ensure guest safety and hygiene
  • Honor confirmed bookings

4. Account & Access Rules

  • Accounts are for personal use only
  • Sharing login credentials is prohibited
  • Fake or duplicate accounts are not allowed

5. Content & Communication Policy

  • No abusive or offensive content
  • No fake reviews or misleading information
  • Harassment or scams result in permanent bans

6. Legal & Policy Updates

ExploreEra may update these policies at any time. Continued use of the platform means acceptance of updated terms.
